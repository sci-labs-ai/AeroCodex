## Aerospace and space terminology sources

These are candidate source registries for controlled vocabulary, glossary, acronym, and standards terminology ingestion. During formal adoption, re-check each URL, license/reuse terms, current version, and project applicability before bulk importing records.

- NASA Thesaurus: https://catalog.data.gov/dataset/nasa-thesaurus
- NASA acronym/glossary pages: https://science.nasa.gov/acronyms/
- ECSS definitions and abbreviated terms: https://ecss.nl/glossary/glossary-definitions-abbreviated-terms/
- FAA abbreviations and acronyms: https://www.faa.gov/about/abbreviations
- FAA AIM Appendix 3 abbreviations and acronyms: https://www.faa.gov/air_traffic/publications/atpubs/aim_html/appendix_3.html
- CCSDS publications and standards: https://ccsds.org/
- DoD Terminology Program: https://www.jcs.mil/doctrine/dod-terminology-program/
- AIAA nomenclature guidance: https://aiaa.org/publications/journals/journal-author/nomenclature-guidelines/

## Adoption note

External terminology should be captured as source-scoped evidence first. It should not become `approved` AeroCodex terminology until a reviewer confirms authority, scope, version, and applicability.
