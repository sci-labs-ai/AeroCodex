## Queue rules

- Do not merge all parallel handoffs together.
- Do not start Session D, Session C, or any other substantive chunk from this document alone.
- Advisory expected count deltas are not commitments. Compute them live from the exact candidate diff and verifiers in the authorized chunk.
- `ready_for_live_intake` means eligible for a future bounded deployment prompt only. It does not mean approved, deployed, validated for operations, or safe to combine with another patch.
- `needs_review` means technically accessible but unresolved semantic, mathematical, governance, dependency, count-delta, or scope questions remain.
- `blocked` means the item is not a deployment candidate in the current authorization boundary.
- `superseded` is reserved for explicit evidence of a newer canonical replacement; it is not used by assumption.
